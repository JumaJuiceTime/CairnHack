# Fever Swamp

- Original system-neutral [adventure](https://www.drivethrurpg.com/product/224803/Fever-Swamp) by [Luke Gearing](https://lukegearing.blot.im/).
- Original conversion by [Danger Is Real](https://dangerisreal.blogspot.com/).

## Diseases
All references to Constitution or Strength are considered STR, and any mental abilities or effects are considered PRS.

## Hexes

### Hex 2 Ur-Corpse Ruins
#### Iron Sinner
9 HP, 2 Armor, 14 STR, 8 AGL, 18 PRS
- **Askguurd**: Zweihander (d6+d6), on critical damage wounds freeze (_deprived_ if target recovers)
- **Naldden**: Truncheon (d8), never kills
- **Disnat**: 6 AGL, 3 Armor, always fails AGL save at start of battle, Short sword (d8)
- **Kaldik**: Spiked chain strangles target unless they follow his commands (d8 STR damage), AGL save to avoid
- **Jondirr**: Broken spear (d6), mask can paralyze those who are not “lecherous” (PRS save to avoid)
- **Tantin**: Paired hammers (d6+d6), 3 AGL (crawls)
- **Dinet**: Giant knife (d12)
- **Naggtet**: Giant hammer d10, every d4 rounds falls and crushes nearby PCs (d8+d8 damage, _blast_)
- Each weapon take up four slots and attack is _impaired_ if wielded by a PC

#### Crocodile Ghoul

#### Ur-corpse
18 HP, 9 AGL, 18 STR, 18 PRS, Claws (d12)
- Critical damage: Necrosis (d8 STR loss)

18 HP, 3 Armor, 18 STR, Fist (d12+d12 _blast_), _detachment_

1 HP per root, 16 STR, PRS 18, Root Crush (d8, _blast_)

#### Trunk Snake
8 HP, 13 STR, 14 AGL, 12 PRS smash (1d6) or Bite (d4)
