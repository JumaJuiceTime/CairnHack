# Night of Blood

- [Night of Blood](https://cubicle7games.com/night-of-blood-pdf) by Jim Bambra
- Conversion by [Joe Burrito](https://github.com/JoeBurrito) 

### Mutant
4 HP, 10 STR, 6 AGL, 6 PRS, dagger or club (d6)

### Beastman
6 HP, 1 Armor, 15 STR, 8 AGL, 8 PRS, sword, bite or claw (d6)

### Hans
6 HP, 1 Armor, 10 STR, 10 AGL, 10 PRS, sword (d8)

### Grat
8 HP, 8 STR, 14 AGL, 6 PRS, bite (d6)

### Otto
2 HP, 10 STR, 4 AGL, 8 PRS, fist (d4)

### Fagor
4 HP, 8 STR, 12 AGL, 8 PRS, knife (d6)

### Wilhelm
6 HP, 11 STR, 10 AGL, 8 PRS, sword (d8)

### Horror
12 HP, 15 STR, 14 AGL, 14 PRS, claws (d8 + d8), Fear - characters must make a PRS check before rolling for initiative or lose one round in fear

### Split horror
12 HP, 13 STR, 16 AGL, 14 PRS, claws (d8 + d8)
