# A Gathering of Blades

- Converted from the original [work by Ben Milton](https://www.patreon.com/questingbeast).

### Commander Hammet
12 HP, Armor 2, 12 STR, 11 AGL, 13 , longsword (d8), throwing knife (d6)

### Sid Clayborne
9 HP, Armor 1, 13 STR, 14 AGL, 9 , longbow (d8), two daggers (d6+d6)

### Soldiers
8 HP, Armor 1, 11 STR, 9 AGL, 7 , Sword (d6) or crossbow (d8), detachment

### Vivan Marsh
4 HP, 5 STR, 9 AGL, 15 

### Travelling Merchant
3 HP, 5 STR, 9 AGL, 13 

### Bandits
7 HP, 9 STR, 13 AGL, 3 , Sword (d6) or bow (d6)

### Wolves
3 HP, 12 STR, 15 AGL, 6 , bite (d8)

### The Lepidoracy
18 HP, 16 STR, 5 AGL, 6 , multi-sword-slash (d12+d12, blast)
- Critical damage: target is eviscerated, and their swords join the awakening.

### Gelatinous King (Giant Skeleton)
15 HP, 14 STR, 9 AGL, 12 , Rawg (d12, blast)
- Only bone-breaking attacks can harm it.
- Critical damage: target is absorbed into its slimy interior for later digestion.

### Gelatinous King
4 HP, 5 STR, 7 AGL, 12 , acidic spray (d6, corrodes armor)

### Rawg, a 10ft sentient sword
- Awaits a true master; shrinks to the size of its latest wielder.
