# Sea Hag

6 HP, 11 STR, 15 AGL, 14 PRS, long claws (d6+d6)

- Ghostly, green-skinned hags that dwell in seaweed forests and warm shallow waters.
- Immune to magic from spellbooks. 
- **Hideous Gaze**: A target is overcome with fear (HP drops to 0). PRS save each round to break the spell.
