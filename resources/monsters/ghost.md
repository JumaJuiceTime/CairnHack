# Ghost

8 HP, 14 STR, 12 AGL, 15 PRS, life drain (d6)

- Incorporeal spirits of the restless dead, vulnerable only to magic and specific anointed rites.
- **Critical Damage**: Target must save PRS or is possessed, acting only on behalf of the ghost's whims.
