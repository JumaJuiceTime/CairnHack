# Green Dragon

12 HP, 2 Armor, 14 STR, 15 AGL, 18 PRS, bite (d12), _detachment_

- Forest-dwelling snakes topped with tall green scales. Collect great hoards of treasure.
- Use intelligence and cunning to manipulate the weak into their service.
- **Chlorine Gas**: Anyone nearby must make a AGL save to escape its reach or lose 1d4 STR and becomes _deprived_.
